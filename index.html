Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to study abroad? +
Costs vary significantly by country and university. USA typically costs $30,000–$60,000/year, UK £15,000–£35,000/year, Canada CAD $20,000–$40,000/year, and Australia AUD $20,000–$45,000/year. We'll give you a realistic budget breakdown in your free consultation.
What loan options are available? +
We work with 20+ banks and NBFCs including SBI, Axis, HDFC Credila, Avanse, and InCred. Collateral-free loans up to ₹50 lakhs are available. We help you compare interest rates and processing fees to find the best option for your situation.
What is your visa success rate? +
Our overall visa success rate is 98% across all countries. We achieve this through meticulous documentation review, strong SOP alignment with visa requirements, and thorough mock interview preparation. We've handled 500+ visa applications this year alone.
How early should I start the process? +
Ideally 12–18 months before your intended start date. This gives time for test prep (IELTS/GRE), shortlisting, SOP drafting, application submission, and visa processing. However, we've also helped students in fast-track scenarios of 6 months.
What GRE score do I need for US universities? +
For top 50 US universities, a GRE score of 315–330 is generally expected. However, your CGPA, work experience, research publications, and SOP carry equal weight. We evaluate your complete profile before making recommendations.
Do you guarantee university admission? +
No ethical consultant can guarantee admission as final decisions rest with universities. However, we build a strategic shortlist across safety, moderate, and ambitious tiers to maximize your probability of receiving at least 3–5 admits.
